Features

Why Safqa?

Complete solution for tracking invoices, managing customers, and smart automatic bank transfer matching

Invoice & Sales Management

Create invoices easily and quickly, track each invoice status (paid / pending / partial), and get daily and monthly sales reports.

Smart Bank Transfer Matching

Import bank statements (PDF, CSV, Excel), automatic smart matching between transfers and invoices with up to 95% accuracy.

Customer Management

Comprehensive customer database, contact information (phone, address, notes), and complete history of all transactions for each customer.

Clear Accounting System

Record debts and deposits accurately, automatic remaining balance calculation, and late debt alerts with detailed account statement.

Works Offline

Offline First - All functions work without internet! Perfect for areas with frequent outages, with automatic sync when internet is available.

Data Security & Privacy

On-device data encryption, password or fingerprint protection, and your data is never sent to external servers - complete control over your privacy.
